## Application Process

### How to Apply

1. Visit the Bureau Boudoir website
2. Click **Apply Now** or navigate to `/signup`
3. Fill in application form:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Experience level (Beginner, Intermediate, Expert)
4. Submit application
5. You'll receive confirmation email immediately

### What Happens Next

**Review Timeline**: 2-3 business days

The admin team will review your application based on:
- Profile completeness
- Experience level
- Fit with Bureau Boudoir brand

## Booking Your Meeting

### Why the Meeting?

The introductory meeting is a crucial step where we:
- Get to know you personally
- Discuss your goals and expectations
- Answer your questions about Bureau Boudoir
- Explain the content creation process
- Review the onboarding steps ahead

### How to Book

1. Log into your dashboard
2. Navigate to **Meetings** tab
3. View available time slots from assigned manager
4. Select preferred:
   - Date
   - Time
   - Meeting type (Online or In-Person)
5. Add optional notes
6. Click **Book Meeting**

### Meeting Confirmation

After booking:
- Receive confirmation email immediately
- Meeting details saved in dashboard
- Can reschedule if needed (up to 24 hours before)
- Manager receives notification

### Before Your Meeting

**Prepare:**
- Review your application details
- List questions you want to ask
- Have ID ready (for age verification)
- Ensure stable internet connection (if online)

**For Online Meetings:**
- Meeting link provided in confirmation email
- Test your camera and microphone beforehand
- Join 5 minutes early

**For In-Person Meetings:**
- Address provided in confirmation
- Arrive on time
- Bring government-issued ID

## Contract Signing

### When You'll Receive Contract

After completing all 10 onboarding steps:
1. Admin reviews your profile
2. Custom contract generated for you
3. Receive email notification
4. Contract available in dashboard

### Contract Details

Your contract includes:
- **Revenue Split**: Your percentage vs. Bureau Boudoir
- **Contract Term**: Duration (typically 6-12 months)
- **Start Date**: When agreement begins
- **Auto-Renewal**: Whether contract auto-renews
- **Termination**: Notice period required
- **Post-Termination Rights**: Platform usage after contract ends
- **Custom Clauses**: Any special agreements

### How to Sign

1. Navigate to **Contract** tab in dashboard
2. Click **Review Contract**
3. Read all terms carefully
4. Download PDF for your records
5. Draw your digital signature
6. Click **Sign Contract**
7. Receive signed copy via email

### After Signing

Once contract is signed:
- ‚úÖ Full creator status activated
- ‚úÖ Can start uploading content
- ‚úÖ Weekly commitments begin
- ‚úÖ Revenue tracking starts
- ‚úÖ Access to all platform features

## FAQ

**Q: How long does onboarding take?**  
A: Typically 1-2 weeks from application to contract signing, depending on your pace.

**Q: Can I skip onboarding steps?**  
A: No, all steps are required for comprehensive profile setup.

**Q: Can I change my stage name later?**  
A: Yes, but requires admin approval and platform updates.

**Q: What if I'm uncomfortable with a commitment?**  
A: Contact your manager immediately. We never force creators into uncomfortable situations.

**Q: Can I work with Bureau Boudoir exclusively?**  
A: Contract terms vary. Discuss exclusivity during your meeting.

**Q: What equipment do I need?**  
A: Minimum: Smartphone with good camera. Recommended: DSLR, ring light, tripod.

**Q: Do I need to live in Amsterdam?**  
A: Studio shoots require Amsterdam presence, but remote content creation possible.

**Q: How are payments processed?**  
A: Monthly invoices based on revenue split, paid via bank transfer.
